Buffalo Chicken

Ingredients needed

  • 1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup cooked chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup ranch or blue cheese salad dressing
  • 2 cups shredded Colby-Monterey jack cheese

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 350 degree. Spread cream cheese into an ungreased shallow 1-qt. baking dish. Layer with chicken, wing sause
  • Bake, uncovered, 20-25 minutes or until cheese is melted. Serve with baguette slices

Test Kitchen Tips

  • Shredded rotisserie chicken works well
  • If you want an ooeey-gooey top with very little browning, cover with foil when baking.
